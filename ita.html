"Tax Assessment - Overview

Income tax assessment is the process by which the income tax department determines the amount of income tax you owe for a particular financial year. This is done by reviewing the information you provide in your income tax return (ITR).

What is an Income Tax Assessment Order?

An Income Tax Assessment Order is a formal document issued by the Income Tax Department after they've reviewed your Income Tax Return (ITR) for a specific financial year. It's the final word on your tax liability for that year, acting as a report card from the department.

The assessment order typically includes the following:

1. Your assessed total income: This is the sum of all your taxable income from various sources like salary, business income, capital gains, etc.

2. Deductions and exemptions allowed: Any deductions or exemptions you claimed in your ITR will be listed here, along with their specific amounts.

3. Taxable income: Based on your taxable income and the prevailing tax slabs for that year, the order will specify the tax rate you're liable for.

4. Applicable tax rate: Based on your taxable income and the prevailing tax slabs for that year, the order will specify the tax rate you're liable for.

5. Tax payable/refund due: This is the final figure – the amount of tax you need to pay to the government if your tax liability exceeds the tax already deducted at source (TDS) or the amount you're due as a refund if the TDS was higher than your actual tax liability.

What is an Assessment Year In Income Tax?

In income tax, the assessment year refers to the 12-month period during which your income earned in the previous financial year is assessed and taxes are calculated. It's the timeframe for settling your tax affairs for a specific financial year.

Financial Year: This is from 1 April of one year to 31 March of the next year when you earn your income.For example, the current financial year is from 1 April 2024 to 31 March 2025.

Assessment Year: This is the year following the financial year, where your income earned in the previous financial year is assessed, and the tax liability is determined. For the current financial year (1 April 2024 to 31 March 2025), the assessment year will be 2025-26.

Documents Required for Income Tax Assessment

Along with other forms, the following documents should be provided to file your income tax returns online:

General Documents:

1. PAN card

2. Aadhaar card

3. Bank account statements

4. Form 26AS

5. Copies of ITR for relevant assessment years

Income-Specific Documents:

1. Salary: PAN card

2. Business/Profession: Aadhaar card

3. Capital gains: Bank account statements

4. House property: Form 26AS

5. Other sources: Copies of ITR for relevant assessment years
